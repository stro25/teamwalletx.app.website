❓ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about TEAM WALLET X.

What is TEAM WALLET X and how does it help sports teams? +

TEAM WALLET X is a comprehensive sports team management platform that replaces spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and manual admin work.

  • Penalty Tracking: Automated fine calculations, assignments, and payment reminders
  • Financial Management: Complete team budget and transaction tracking
  • Equipment Inventory: Full lifecycle tracking from purchase to retirement
  • Team Communication: Announcements, votings, and integrated chat
  • Gamification: Achievements, leaderboards, and clean sheet tracking
  • Season Management: Close seasons, roll over data, browse archives
  • Role Feature Hubs: 10 role-specific premium toolkits (Lineup Builder, Injury Tracker, Meal Plans, and more)
How much does TEAM WALLET X cost? +

TEAM WALLET X offers role-based pricing so you only pay for the features you need:

  • Player: €1.99/month (€19.99/year) — Personal stats, achievements, team chat, Personal Goals hub
  • Staff: €3.99/month (€39.99/year) — Team analytics, calendar management, role-specific hub
  • Coach: €5.99/month (€59.99/year) — Lineup Builder, Training Attendance, penalty management
  • Admin: €7.99/month (€79.99/year) — Full financial control, season management, Budget Planner hub
  • TEAM: €19.99/month (€149.99/year) — One admin pays, every team member (up to 50) gets full premium access automatically

Yearly plans save you 2 months compared to monthly billing.

Is there a free version or free trial? +

Yes! TEAM WALLET X includes a free tier with core functionality:

  • View penalties, team calendar, and announcements
  • View and request equipment
  • View personal balance
  • Up to 16 team members and 10 penalties

Upgrade to any paid tier when you're ready to unlock the full feature set.

What's the difference between the four roles? +

Each role builds on the one below, with increasing levels of team management access:

  • Player: Personal stats, achievements, and team communication
  • Staff: Equipment management, calendar events, basic analytics
  • Coach: Penalty assignment, unlimited team size, advanced reporting
  • Admin: Full financial control, user permissions, all exports

Users can hold multiple roles simultaneously — perfect for player-captains or coach-admins.

How does the penalty tracking system work? +

The automated penalty system handles the entire fine lifecycle:

  • Coaches create custom categories (late arrival, missed training, red card...)
  • Coaches assign fines to players with one tap
  • Players receive push notification reminders
  • Admins mark penalties as paid and generate financial reports
  • Full audit trail with timestamps and history
How does the Equipment & Inventory Management work? +

The complete inventory system covers every aspect of equipment management:

  • Track every item with photos, sizes, conditions, and purchase info
  • Assign equipment to players with due dates and return tracking
  • Players can request equipment with size and priority
  • Staff approve/deny requests and resolve issues
  • AI-powered item scanner for quick inventory additions
  • Severity-based issue reporting (Damaged / Lost / Defective)
Is my team's data secure? +

Absolutely. TEAM WALLET X implements enterprise-grade security:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest
  • Full GDPR compliance with data export and deletion options
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID)
  • Role-based access so users only see relevant data
  • Secure keychain management on device
What are Role Feature Hubs and how do they work? +

Role Feature Hubs are dedicated premium toolkits unlocked by your role subscription — purpose-built for the way you work in the team:

  • Admin: Budget Planner — track team income and expenses with full financial oversight
  • Player: Personal Goals — set, track, and achieve individual performance targets
  • Coach: Lineup Builder (linked to calendar matches) & Training Attendance logging with Select All
  • Assistant Coach: Player Focus Notes — private, per-player development notes
  • Manager: Contract List — player contracts, strengths, and weaknesses, fully editable
  • Physiotherapist: Injury Tracker — severity levels, recovery timelines, return-to-play status
  • Team Doctor: Allergy & Condition Registry — secure per-player medical records
  • Kit Manager: Pre-Match Checklists, AI Inventory Insights, Custom Types, Kit Assignments & Requests
  • Analyst: Match Event Logs — detailed in-game event recording per player
  • Nutritionist: Meal Plan Templates — create and assign nutrition plans to players

All hubs are accessible from the Profile tab's scrollable role carousel. A premium subscription is required to enter any hub.

How does Season Management work? +

Season Management lets admins structure the team around a football season and seamlessly transition to the next:

  • Active Season: All app data (penalties, finances, equipment, calendar) reflects the currently selected season
  • Close Season Wizard: A guided wizard walks the admin through rolling over members, wallet balances, and equipment at the end of the year
  • Wallet Carryover Choice: Admin decides whether to carry over player wallet balances into the new season as an opening entry, or reset to €0
  • Season Archives (Premium): Past seasons are available as read-only archives — every penalty, financial report, and statistic preserved
  • Season Champion Badge: The top-ranked player when a season closes earns a permanent Champion badge
  • Season Switcher: Switch between seasons from the Home tab — all tabs update automatically to reflect the selected season's data

Browsing past seasons is a premium feature. Season management itself (creating and closing) is available to all admin users.

Can a user hold multiple roles simultaneously? +

Yes — TEAM WALLET X fully supports multi-role users. A player can also be a coach, or an admin can hold a secondary specialist role like Physiotherapist or Analyst.

  • Admins are required to have at least one secondary role by design
  • All assigned roles unlock their respective Feature Hubs simultaneously
  • A scrollable Role Carousel in the Profile tab lets you navigate between all your role hubs
  • Roles are assigned by the admin and visible to the full team
Is TEAM WALLET X available on Android? +

Currently, TEAM WALLET X is available exclusively on iOS (iPhone and iPad), requiring iOS 17.0 or later.

We focused on iOS first to deliver the best possible experience with native performance, seamless integration with iOS features, and premium design quality. Android expansion is being evaluated based on user demand.

❓ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about TEAM WALLET X.

What is TEAM WALLET X and how does it help sports teams? +

TEAM WALLET X is a comprehensive sports team management platform that replaces spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and manual admin work.

  • Penalty Tracking: Automated fine calculations, assignments, and payment reminders
  • Financial Management: Complete team budget and transaction tracking
  • Equipment Inventory: Full lifecycle tracking from purchase to retirement
  • Team Communication: Announcements, votings, and integrated chat
  • Gamification: Achievements, leaderboards, and clean sheet tracking
  • Season Management: Close seasons, roll over data, browse archives
  • Role Feature Hubs: 10 role-specific premium toolkits (Lineup Builder, Injury Tracker, Meal Plans, and more)
How much does TEAM WALLET X cost? +

TEAM WALLET X offers role-based pricing so you only pay for the features you need:

  • Player: €1.99/month (€19.99/year) — Personal stats, achievements, team chat
  • Staff: €3.99/month (€39.99/year) — Team analytics, calendar management
  • Coach: €5.99/month (€59.99/year) — Advanced tools, penalty management
  • Admin: €7.99/month (€79.99/year) — Full financial control, priority support

Yearly plans save you 2 months compared to monthly billing.

Is there a free version or free trial? +

Yes! TEAM WALLET X includes a free tier with core functionality:

  • View penalties, team calendar, and announcements
  • View and request equipment
  • View personal balance
  • Up to 16 team members and 10 penalties

Upgrade to any paid tier when you're ready to unlock the full feature set.

What's the difference between the four roles? +

Each role builds on the one below, with increasing levels of team management access:

  • Player: Personal stats, achievements, and team communication
  • Staff: Equipment management, calendar events, basic analytics
  • Coach: Penalty assignment, unlimited team size, advanced reporting
  • Admin: Full financial control, user permissions, all exports

Users can hold multiple roles simultaneously — perfect for player-captains or coach-admins.

How does the penalty tracking system work? +

The automated penalty system handles the entire fine lifecycle:

  • Coaches create custom categories (late arrival, missed training, red card...)
  • Coaches assign fines to players with one tap
  • Players receive push notification reminders
  • Admins mark penalties as paid and generate financial reports
  • Full audit trail with timestamps and history
How does the Equipment & Inventory Management work? +

The complete inventory system covers every aspect of equipment management:

  • Track every item with photos, sizes, conditions, and purchase info
  • Assign equipment to players with due dates and return tracking
  • Players can request equipment with size and priority
  • Staff approve/deny requests and resolve issues
  • AI-powered item scanner for quick inventory additions
  • Severity-based issue reporting (Damaged / Lost / Defective)
Is my team's data secure? +

Absolutely. TEAM WALLET X implements enterprise-grade security:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest
  • Full GDPR compliance with data export and deletion options
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID)
  • Role-based access so users only see relevant data
  • Secure keychain management on device
What are Role Feature Hubs and how do they work? +

Role Feature Hubs are dedicated premium toolkits unlocked by your role subscription — purpose-built for the way you work in the team:

  • Admin: Budget Planner — track team income and expenses with full financial oversight
  • Player: Personal Goals — set, track, and achieve individual performance targets
  • Coach: Lineup Builder (linked to calendar matches) & Training Attendance logging with Select All
  • Assistant Coach: Player Focus Notes — private, per-player development notes
  • Manager: Contract List — player contracts, strengths, and weaknesses, fully editable
  • Physiotherapist: Injury Tracker — severity levels, recovery timelines, return-to-play status
  • Team Doctor: Allergy & Condition Registry — secure per-player medical records
  • Kit Manager: Pre-Match Checklists, AI Inventory Insights, Custom Types, Kit Assignments & Requests
  • Analyst: Match Event Logs — detailed in-game event recording per player
  • Nutritionist: Meal Plan Templates — create and assign nutrition plans to players

All hubs are accessible from the Profile tab's scrollable role carousel. A premium subscription is required to enter any hub.

How does Season Management work? +

Season Management lets admins structure the team around a football season and seamlessly transition to the next:

  • Active Season: All app data (penalties, finances, equipment, calendar) reflects the currently selected season
  • Close Season Wizard: A guided wizard walks the admin through rolling over members, wallet balances, and equipment at the end of the year
  • Wallet Carryover Choice: Admin decides whether to carry over player wallet balances into the new season as an opening entry, or reset to €0
  • Season Archives (Premium): Past seasons are available as read-only archives — every penalty, financial report, and statistic preserved
  • Season Champion Badge: The top-ranked player when a season closes earns a permanent Champion badge
  • Season Switcher: Switch between seasons from the Home tab — all tabs update automatically to reflect the selected season's data

Browsing past seasons is a premium feature. Season management itself (creating and closing) is available to all admin users.

Can a user hold multiple roles simultaneously? +

Yes — TEAM WALLET X fully supports multi-role users. A player can also be a coach, or an admin can hold a secondary specialist role like Physiotherapist or Analyst.

  • Admins are required to have at least one secondary role by design
  • All assigned roles unlock their respective Feature Hubs simultaneously
  • A scrollable Role Carousel in the Profile tab lets you navigate between all your role hubs
  • Roles are assigned by the admin and visible to the full team
Is TEAM WALLET X available on Android? +

Currently, TEAM WALLET X is available exclusively on iOS (iPhone and iPad), requiring iOS 17.0 or later.

We focused on iOS first to deliver the best possible experience with native performance, seamless integration with iOS features, and premium design quality. Android expansion is being evaluated based on user demand.
